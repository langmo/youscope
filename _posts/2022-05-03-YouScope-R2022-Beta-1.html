--- layout: layout title: YouScope R2022 Beta 1 (v2.2.0-beta.1) released ---

A wihle after the last official release of YouScope comes the first beta version of the R2022 release.
This pre-release is built against all latest state of the art device drivers. This major change focuses on the 64-bit platform. We added support for automatic refilling and/or switching of Nemesys pumps from a reservoir. Also, a few new features for the GUI are in preparation and will be released soon! Try it out!
